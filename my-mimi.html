In Loving Memory of

Rosellie J. Colandog

(1996 – 2024)

“Once given so deeply, love never fades and never loses its place—it lives on in every heartbeat and every memory. The bond we share is eternal, beyond time and space. Our connection transcends both life and death, leaving only remnants between us. We were destined to cross paths, but not bound by fate to stay. Until we meet again, for now, I shall live, love, and carry you in my heart forever.”
